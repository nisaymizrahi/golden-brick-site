About Golden Brick Construction

Golden Brick Construction provides renovation, construction, and project management services for homeowners and real estate investors across Philadelphia and surrounding suburbs.

Built on Real Construction Experience

Golden Brick Construction works with homeowners and real estate investors on serious renovation and construction projects across Philadelphia.

Our work includes kitchen and bathroom renovations, full gut rehabs, multifamily renovations, rental property upgrades, and new residential construction.

With more than 25 years of experience in Philadelphia real estate, we understand both the construction side and the investment strategy behind successful projects.

What We Do Residential renovations, investor renovation projects, multifamily rehabs, full gut renovations, and new construction.
Who We Work With Homeowners planning renovations and real estate investors developing flip or rental projects.
Service Area Philadelphia and surrounding suburbs across Bucks, Montgomery, and Delaware counties.
