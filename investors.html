Our Investor Report Package

One of the most valuable things we provide investors is a more complete picture of the deal. For sourced properties, owned properties, or properties an investor is considering purchasing, we help assemble a professional report package to support decision-making.

This package is designed to help investors assess feasibility, understand renovation scope, review value potential, and compare strategies before moving forward.

Rather than relying on guesswork, investors receive a more structured evaluation that combines comps, scope, projected numbers, and strategy insight based on real local experience.

What the Report Can Include

  • As-is comps report for the current property condition
  • Construction estimate tied to the proposed scope
  • ARV comps report based on the planned improvements
  • Projected income and expense review
  • Rental comps and cap rate support
  • Strategy discussion based on property type and location
  • Key issues to watch out for before moving forward
  • Guidance for flip, rental, multifamily, or rent-by-room positioning

Investor FAQ

Questions Investors Ask Before They Move Forward

These are the practical topics that usually come up before an investor requests a review, estimate, or project conversation.

Can you review a deal before I buy it?

Yes. Reviewing the property before purchase is one of the most valuable stages for investor clients because it helps frame scope, risk, renovation strategy, and whether the deal fits the intended exit.

Do you only work with flips?

No. We work with flips, long-term rentals, multifamily repositioning, rent-by-room concepts, and new construction projects when the scope and goals are a fit.

Can you help with scope and budget before the plans are final?

Yes. Many investor conversations begin before every detail is finalized. We can help shape the renovation direction, identify scope drivers, and prepare a more realistic framework for the numbers.

Do you coordinate with lenders, designers, and management partners?

Yes. We regularly work alongside trusted partners when a project needs lending introductions, planning help, specialty design input, or post-renovation management support.

What areas do you cover for investor work?

We focus on Philadelphia and surrounding Pennsylvania suburbs, including Bucks, Montgomery, Delaware, and nearby areas where the project fits the scope and strategy.
