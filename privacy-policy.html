Privacy Policy

How We Handle Project Inquiry Information

This page explains how Golden Brick Construction collects, uses, stores, and communicates with people who submit project inquiries, consultation requests, and contact forms through the website.

Effective March 28, 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact Golden Brick Construction, we may collect the information you choose to provide, including your name, email address, phone number, project address, project type, timeline, communication preferences, and any project details you submit through our forms, calls, emails, or other communications.

How We Use Your Information

  • To review project inquiries and provide free consultation follow-up.
  • To contact you by phone, text message, or email about your request, project scope, scheduling, and next steps.
  • To prepare estimates, review project fit, and coordinate with internal staff or service providers supporting the inquiry process.
  • To send marketing emails, promotional texts, or similar updates only when you separately opt in to those communications.
  • To improve our website, lead handling, and customer service process.

Communication Consent

If you submit a project inquiry or free consultation request, you consent to Golden Brick Construction contacting you about that request using the contact information you provide, including phone, text, and email where appropriate.

Marketing or promotional emails, calls, and text messages are optional and are based on the additional choices you make on our forms. Consent to receive marketing communications is not a condition of purchase.

How To Opt Out

You can ask us to stop marketing communications at any time by contacting us at info@goldenbrickc.com or calling (267) 715-5557. If we send a message with unsubscribe or opt-out instructions, you may also use those instructions.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website, process inquiries, store records, or communicate with customers, but only as reasonably necessary to support our business operations.

Data Retention And Protection

We keep inquiry records for as long as reasonably needed for business, operational, legal, or recordkeeping purposes. While no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, we use reasonable steps to protect information submitted through the website and our internal systems.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your communication preferences, contact Golden Brick Construction at info@goldenbrickc.com or (267) 715-5557.

At A Glance

  • Inquiry follow-up is tied to the information you submit.
  • Marketing emails and promotional texts should only be sent when the visitor opts in.
  • Opt-out requests should be honored promptly.
  • Project inquiries can still be handled even if someone declines marketing updates.
